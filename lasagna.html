LASAGNA!

Description

This is delicious lasagna. It has noodles and meat and pasta and sauce and cheese. It is also hot. WHAT ELSE COULD YOU WANT!?

Ingrediates

  • NOODLES
  • MEAT
  • PASTA (maybe covered by 'noodles')
  • SAUCE
  • CHESSE

Steps:

  1. Make the Noodles in HOT WATER. They better be squishy!
  2. Cook the meat until it is cooked.
  3. Pasta is definitely the same as noodles - disregard the pasta but keep the noodles.
  4. Make the sauce hot. I don't know if this is necessary, but it seems right!
  5. Be ready with cheese.
  6. Get a big pan - a lasagna pan.
  7. Delicately layer pasta, sauce, meat, and cheese so several layers of each are stacked, with the final, top layer being sauce to cover.
  8. Sprinkle mo cheese all up top of that.
  9. Cook it until it's cooked.
